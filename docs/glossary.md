# Glossary

- **AUC**: Area under the ROC curve; measures ranking quality across thresholds.
- **Precision**: Share of predicted positives that are truly positive.
- **Recall**: Share of true positives correctly identified.
- **False Positive**: Case flagged positive that is actually negative.
- **Default Rate**: Portion of borrowers who default in a defined period.
- **Delinquency**: Late payment status relative to contract terms.
- **PAR30 / PAR90**: Portfolio at risk with payments overdue by 30/90+ days.
- **Scorecard**: Structured scoring approach using interpretable weighted features.
- **Decision Engine**: System that combines rules/models to produce actions.
- **Explainability**: Ability to justify why a system made a decision.
- **Data Drift**: Change in data distribution over time.
- **Model Monitoring**: Ongoing checks for performance, drift, and stability.
