# Responsible AI Notes

## Practical guidance for fintech and analytics apps

- Prefer synthetic data for demos and public repositories.
- Avoid features that may encode protected or proxy discriminatory signals.
- Govern threshold changes with documented approval and rationale.
- Maintain audit logs of model/rule versions and key decisions.
- Keep a human review path for high-impact outcomes.
- Be transparent about model limitations and uncertainty.

Responsible AI is not a checklist-only exercise; it is continuous operational practice.
