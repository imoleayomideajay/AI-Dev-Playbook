# Security Policy

## Supported scope

This repository is primarily documentation and starter templates. Security issues may still affect examples, dependencies, or guidance.

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please do **not** open public issues for suspected vulnerabilities.

Instead, email: `security@ai-dev-playbook.example`

Include:
- Description of the issue
- Affected file(s) or template(s)
- Reproduction steps or proof of concept
- Potential impact
- Suggested mitigation (if known)

## Response expectations

- Initial acknowledgment: within 3 business days
- Triage and next steps: within 7 business days when possible
- Coordinated disclosure preferred after remediation

## Responsible disclosure

We appreciate responsible disclosure and ask researchers to avoid data destruction, service disruption, or unauthorized access attempts.
